NEW PAPER SKELETON — written 2026-04-29
=========================================

This file is a checkpoint of the new paper framing AFTER the failed
"compression solves CL" framing was abandoned and replaced with the
"compression discovers Fisher information for free" framing. Read this
first if context has compacted.

WORKING TITLE
-------------
"Self-Compression as a Free Importance Signal for Continual Learning"
(or) "Discovering Fisher Information through Differentiable Compression"

WHAT FAILED (so we don't accidentally re-pursue):
-------------------------------------------------
1. "CSC alone solves CL" — empirically wrong. CSC-alone (no replay/DER, no EWC):
   36.12 ± 2.95 ≈ Finetune 36.50 ± 3.29. Soft-protect's gradient scaling is
   too uniform at γ=10⁻³ (bit-depth distribution narrow: mean 6.79 of 8,
   std 0.75, all in [5.96, 8.0]).

2. "Smart bit allocation across tasks" — empirically dead. No freed channels,
   no specialists, no bimodality. The compression dynamics produce a graded
   but narrow distribution, not a specialised one.

3. "Capacity-efficiency wrapper" — beaten by trivial PTQ baseline. Naive 8-bit
   PTQ of DER++: 81.02 ≈ CSC+DER++ 81.24. CSC operates entirely in the
   precision regime where uniform PTQ is essentially lossless.

4. "Higher γ_comp would help" — Csefalvay quantiser has b=0 absorbing state.
   Once a channel hits 0 output, task gradient ⇒ 0, no recovery. Higher γ
   produces irreversible pruning, not specialisation.

5. "Method modification (γ schedule)" — BLIP (Shi et al. CVPR 2021) already
   does per-task bit allocation with hard freezing. Reviewer pushback would
   be immediate.

WHAT SURVIVED (the new core)
----------------------------
1. **Fisher correlation** (the cleanest novel finding):
   - rho(b, F) = +0.832 ± 0.026 across 3 seeds, R18 / Split CIFAR-100, t=0.
   - Partial Spearman controlling for |w|: rho(b, F | |w|) = +0.826 ± 0.026.
   - The +0.006 drop confirms it's NOT a magnitude artefact.
   - Csc_bd_ewc (replace EWC's Fisher with bit-depth) reaches 46.7% vs
     CSC-alone 36.1% vs EWC-with-real-Fisher 72.5%. Bit-depth captures
     real but partial Fisher information.

2. **Bd warm-start adds +2pp to EWC at every K**:
   - K=1: ewc 72.31 → csc_bd_ewc_warm 74.18  (+1.87)
   - K=2: ewc 72.53 → 74.52  (+1.99)
   - K=5: ewc 72.56 → 74.45  (+1.89)
   - K=10: ewc 72.56 → 74.45  (+1.89)
   - K=20: ewc 71.41 → 74.27  (+2.86)
   Bit-depth carries information COMPLEMENTARY to empirical Fisher.

3. **Bd as omega beats MAS-omega in Online-LoRA by +6.4pp**:
   - Reproduction of Online-LoRA WACV 2025 with minimal CSC hooks:
     olora_mas:     13.82 ± 3.10
     olora_csc_mas: 13.20 ± 0.47   (CSC quant, MAS signal — control)
     olora_csc_bd:  20.17 ± 1.55   (CSC quant, BD signal)
   - csc_mas ≈ baseline → win comes from the SIGNAL, not from compression.
   - CAVEAT: absolute numbers are well below published Online-LoRA (~60%);
     hyperparameters need work (Phase 1 of the new plan).

4. **RL ClonEx-SAC**: independent contribution; CSC composes with BC distill
   in Continual World (csc_clonex 69.5% ± 10.0% vs ClonEx 69.0% ± 9.0% —
   tied; the diagnosis story stands).

NEUROBIO GROUNDING (still load-bearing, now MORE so)
----------------------------------------------------
- Efficient-coding hypothesis (Barlow 1961) + sparse coding (Olshausen &
  Field 1996): networks SHOULD learn compressed representations because
  that's what works. Our finding: compression dynamics produce a
  Fisher-aligned importance signal as a side effect.
- Synaptic homeostasis (Tononi & Cirelli 2014): during sleep, synapses are
  globally down-scaled with selective preservation. Bit-depth is exactly
  this — always-on, no task boundary, selective.
- CLS theory (McClelland 1995): the brain doesn't compute Fisher on a
  hard buffer of 4 samples; it has continuous metabolic precision signals.
- MDL (Hinton & van Camp 1993) + IB (Tishby 2000): minimum description
  length naturally yields parameter importance.

CORE CONTRIBUTIONS (NEW)
------------------------
1. Compression dynamics through differentiable per-channel bit-depth
   produce a learned importance signal correlated with empirical Fisher
   at rho ≈ 0.83 (controlling for weight magnitude). The signal is
   continuously updated at zero extra cost (no per-task Fisher pass).

2. The signal is COMPLEMENTARY to empirical Fisher: bd warm-start lifts
   EWC by ~2pp on Split CIFAR-100 (R18) at every batch budget K ∈ {1..20}.

3. The signal REPLACES MAS-style omega in task-free Online-LoRA on
   ViT-B/16 with +6.4pp accuracy gain over their 4-sample MAS gradient.
   Also a drift detector candidate (untested).

4. The mechanism extends to off-policy continual RL (cw_subset on
   Continual World): csc_clonex matches ClonEx-SAC at zero accuracy cost.

5. Theoretical link from differentiable bit-depth gradient through STE
   to channel-wise Fisher information (Jensen lower bound).
